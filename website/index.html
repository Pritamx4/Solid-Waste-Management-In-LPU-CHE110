Solid Waste Management In LPU report.

  • Introduction

    • The introduction sets the stage for examining solid waste management at Lovely Professional University (LPU) within the context of sustainable development goals (SDGs). LPU, a prominent Indian university, faces the challenge of managing waste effectively in its large and diverse campus. The discussion emphasizes the importance of this task in achieving SDGs, particularly Goal 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities) and Goal 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production). The introduction outlines the broader objectives of sustainable development, introducing the report's focus on LPU's efforts in solid waste management. It signals an upcoming analysis of the university's practices, aiming to showcase their impact on sustainability and provide insights for improvement

    SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT

  • GOAL

    • The goal of sustainable development is to meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. Solid waste, encompassing a range of materials from paper and plastics to organic matter, poses a significant environmental challenge globally. In the context of LPU, the university community's commitment to addressing this challenge becomes crucial in contributing to the larger narrative of sustainable development.

  • SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT PROJECT COMES UNDER THE GOAL OF 7 AND AS WELL AS GOAL 11
  • GOAL 7 – AFFORDABLE AND CLEAN ENERGY

    • The exploration of waste to waste technology represents a significant step forwards achieving SDG7 . By assessing the feasibility and potential implementation of such technologies Lovely Professional University aims to generate renewable energy from waste promoting cleaner and more sustainable energy sources

  • GOAL 11– SUSTAINABLE CITIES AND COMMITIES

    • The recommendation in the report focuses on optimising waste management process fostering community engagement and implementing environmentally responsible practice these efforts contribute to the creation of sustainable and resilient communities within the university
